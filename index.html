About The Team

We are CWRUbotix, Case Western Reserve Universityâ€™s robotics club. We like long walks on the beach (with robots), designing and building robots for a variety of robotics competitions, and engaging with the local STEM community. Our primary goal is to provide a learning environment for students interested in robotics, regardless of prior experience. We focus on teaching our members technical skills such as CAD modeling, circuit design, programming, and manufacturing. Currently we attend NASA's Robotic Mining Competition, an assortment of National Robotics Challenges, and the Xtreme Collegiate Clash tournament.
