Projects

Here at CWRUbotix we do all sorts of different projects. We participate in two national competitions and all things to support robotics and STEM.

NASA Robotics Mining Competition

The NASA Robotics Mining Competition (NASA RMC) is a national collegete competition for which teams design a robot capable of excavating simulated Marsian regolith within a similuted Mars enviroment. The competition is held every year in May at the NASA Kennedy Space Center.

National Robotics Challenge

The National Robotcs Challenge is multiple robotics competitions rolled into one event. Held nearby in Marion, Ohio in the beginning of April, the competitions are open to students in 6th grade through graduate school. In 2016, our teams participated in the Rescue, Combat, Maze, and Sumo competitions.
